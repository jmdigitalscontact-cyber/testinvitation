This is an adults-only celebration, except for children specifically included in the entourage.

FAQ

Common questions answered

How do I RSVP?

Use the link or QR code on your invitation. If you need help, please contact us directly.

Is RSVP required?

Yes, RSVP is required and seating is reserved in advance. Please respond as soon as you can.

Can I bring a plus one?

No. This celebration is strictly by invitation only for listed guests.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony begins at 2:00 PM sharp at St. John Marie Vianney Parish Church. Please arrive early.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

The ceremony is unplugged. Please enjoy the moment and allow the photo team to cover the event.

Where should I park?

Parking is available near the church in Lalaan II, Silang. For the reception, follow venue staff directions at Alta Terra Tagaytay.
